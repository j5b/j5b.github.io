About me

I am a PhD student in the theory group at the Computer Lab in Cambridge, supervised by Prof. Anuj Dawar. I am also a member of Clare College.

My research interests include algorithm design, (parameterized) complexity theory, graph theory and (finite) model theory.

Before coming to Cambridge, I completed a BSc in Mathematics and Computer Science at Imperial College London and an MSc in Mathematics and Foundations of Computer Science at the University of Oxford.
